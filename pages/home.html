Game guide
Battle Brawlers is a strategy auto-battler playable in your browser.

Your main objective is to upgrade your Ludus by obtaining experience and leveling it up.

You can recruit Brawlers to fight for you. Each Brawler can be equipped with unique items and abilities.

Every 5 Ludus levels, you unlock an additional Brawler slot.


Combat
Combat is automatic once you start a fight.

Battle Brawlers uses a tick system. A tick cycle is normally 12 ticks, but it can expand up to 15 ticks. Each ability you place in your Active Abilities has its own tick length, which represents how long the ability takes to cast or remain active.

Tick cycles repeat until a winner is determined.


The arena
There's a vast roster of fights available in the arena. Each fight features its own unique set of enemies and rewards.

Boss fights unlock additional content in Battle Brawlers. But be wary! Luck-based combat stats are disabled during boss fights!
